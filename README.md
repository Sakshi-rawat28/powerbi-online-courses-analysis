# 📊 Online Courses Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an online courses dataset sourced from Kaggle using Power BI. The dataset was cleaned, transformed, and visualized to uncover trends in course popularity, instructor performance, language distribution, ratings, and learner engagement.

The dashboard provides insights into the most popular course categories, top-rated instructors, course languages, and factors influencing learner interest.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze course popularity across categories and sub-categories.
- Identify top-performing instructors based on ratings.
- Explore language distribution among online courses.
- Compare categories based on views, ratings, and skills covered.
- Generate actionable insights through interactive visualizations.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Data Science is the Most Popular Category
Data Science courses received the highest average views (4,401+), indicating strong demand for data-related skills.

### 2. Computer Science Courses Attract Significant Engagement
Computer Science ranked among the top-performing categories with over 4,100 average views.

### 3. English Dominates Course Content
The majority of courses are offered in English, making it the primary language for online learning content.

### 4. Course Type Distribution is Highly Skewed
Standard courses significantly outnumber professional certificates and specializations.

### 5. Instructor Ratings Remain Consistently High
Top instructors maintained ratings around 4.4, indicating strong learner satisfaction.

### 6. Viewer Count Varies Across Categories
Categories such as Data Science and Computer Science outperform others in terms of learner engagement and popularity.

### 7. Course Duration Does Not Guarantee Higher Engagement
Longer courses do not always attract more viewers, suggesting that relevance and content quality play a bigger role than duration alone.
